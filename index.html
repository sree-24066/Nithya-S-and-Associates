Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

What services does Nithya S & Associates provide?
+

We offer a comprehensive range of services including accounting & book-keeping, statutory and internal auditing, tax planning & compliance, GST services, business registration, financial advisory, startup advisory, virtual CFO services, payroll management, and project finance solutions.

How can I schedule a consultation?
+

You can schedule a consultation by calling us at +91 90424 33066, emailing Nithyasandassociates@gmail.com, or by filling out the contact form on our website. We typically respond within 24 hours to arrange a convenient meeting time.

Do you handle GST registration and filing?
+

Yes, we provide complete GST solutions including new registration, amendments, monthly/quarterly return filing (GSTR-1, GSTR-3B), annual returns, GST refund claims, e-way bill management, and compliance advisory.

What industries do you serve?
+

We serve clients across diverse sectors including manufacturing, IT & technology, healthcare, real estate, education, retail & e-commerce, hospitality, agriculture, non-profits, and import/export businesses.

What are your office hours?
+

Our office is open Monday to Saturday from 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays and public holidays. For urgent matters, you can reach us on our phone during extended hours.
