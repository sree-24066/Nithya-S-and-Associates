Our Story

A New Chapter in Financial Excellence

Nithya S & Associates was founded with a clear vision: to provide businesses and individuals with the highest caliber of chartered accountancy and financial advisory services. Under the leadership of CA Nithya S, our firm brings together professional rigor, modern methodologies, and a deeply personal commitment to every client engagement.

Located in the heart of Kuniamuthur, Coimbatore, we serve a diverse clientele spanning multiple industries. Our approach is simple — we treat your financial health with the same care and precision as our own, building lasting relationships grounded in trust, transparency, and tangible results.

As a newly established firm, we combine fresh perspectives with time-tested accounting principles to deliver services that are both innovative and thoroughly reliable.
