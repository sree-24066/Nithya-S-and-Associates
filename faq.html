Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, processes, and how we can help your business.

What services does Nithya S & Associates provide?
+

We offer a comprehensive range of services including accounting & book-keeping, statutory and internal auditing, tax planning & compliance, GST services, business registration, financial advisory, startup advisory, virtual CFO services, payroll management, project finance solutions, and foreign investment advisory.

Where is your office located?
+

Our office is located at KRK Complex, 510/11, Palakkad - Coimbatore Rd, Kuniyamuthur, Coimbatore – 641008. We are easily accessible from all major areas of Coimbatore.

What are your office hours?
+

Our office is open Monday to Saturday from 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays and public holidays. For urgent matters, you can reach us on our phone during extended hours.

How can I schedule a consultation?
+

You can schedule a consultation by calling us at +91 90424 33066, emailing Nithyasandassociates@gmail.com, or by filling out the contact form on our website. We typically respond within 24 hours.

Do you provide services to businesses outside Coimbatore?
+

Yes, we serve clients across Tamil Nadu and other states in India. Many of our services can be delivered digitally, making geographic location a non-barrier. We use cloud-based tools for seamless collaboration.

What industries do you specialize in?
+

We serve diverse sectors including manufacturing, IT & technology, healthcare, real estate, education, retail, hospitality, agriculture, NGOs, import/export, energy, and financial services. Our sector-specific expertise ensures relevant, practical solutions.

Do you offer virtual CFO services?
+

Yes, we provide comprehensive Virtual CFO services for growing businesses. This includes financial strategy, MIS reporting, cash flow management, board-level advisory, fundraising support, and investor relations — without the cost of a full-time CFO.

Can you help with company registration?
+

Absolutely! We handle all types of business registrations — Private Limited Company, LLP, OPC, Partnership Firm, Sole Proprietorship, NGO/Trust registration, MSME/Udyam registration, and trade license acquisition.

Do you provide payroll management services?
+

Yes, we offer end-to-end payroll services including salary computation, PF & ESI management, professional tax, TDS on salary, payslip generation, and complete HR statutory compliance.

Do you handle GST registration and filing?
+

Yes, we provide complete GST lifecycle management — new registration, amendments, monthly/quarterly return filing (GSTR-1, GSTR-3B), annual returns (GSTR-9), GST refund claims, e-way bill management, and compliance advisory.

Can you help me save on taxes legally?
+

Absolutely! Our tax planning services help you legally minimize your tax liability through strategic use of exemptions, deductions, investment planning, and optimal business structuring. We always operate within the boundaries of the law.

What types of audits do you perform?
+

We perform statutory audits, tax audits (under Section 44AB), internal audits, GST audits, management audits, and special purpose audits. Our audit methodology ensures thorough examination and actionable insights.

Can you handle income tax notices?
+

Yes, we assist clients with responding to income tax notices, scrutiny assessments, appeals, and other proceedings before tax authorities. Our experienced team ensures your interests are well represented.

How do you charge for your services?
+

Our pricing is transparent and based on the scope and complexity of the engagement. We offer both fixed-fee packages and hourly billing options. All fees are communicated upfront before engagement begins — no hidden charges.

Do you offer packages for startups?
+

Yes, we have special startup packages that bundle essential services — company registration, GST setup, basic accounting, tax filing, and compliance — at attractive pricing to support emerging businesses.

What is the initial consultation fee?
+

We offer a complimentary initial consultation to understand your needs and recommend the right services. This allows you to assess our expertise and approach before committing to any engagement.
